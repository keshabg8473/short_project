A.P.J abdul kalam

1931-2015

missile man of india

Aval pakir jainalabdeen abdul kalam 15 (oct 1931-27 july 2015) was an indian acrospace scientice who serve as the 11th president of india from 2002 to 2007.He was born and raised in Rameswaram,Tamil nadu and studied physics and acrospace engineering.He spent the next four decades as a scientice and science administration ,mainly at the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and indian space Research Organisation(ISRO)and was intimately involved in india's civilian space programmere and smilitary missile development efforts[1].He thus came to be known as the missile man of india for his work on the development of ballistic missile and lunch vehicle technology [2][3][4]He also played a pivocal organigation,technical,and political role in india's pokhrn-11 nuclear tests in 1998,the first since orgiginal nuclear test by india in 1974.
